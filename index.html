Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Dopple mouse compatible with my gaming setup?
Yes. Dopple connects via USB-C and works seamlessly with Windows, macOS, and Linux. It is plug-and-play for most systems, and our optional config software runs on all major platforms.
How long does the battery last?
You can expect about 70 hours with full RGB on. If you switch to eco mode or disable lighting, the battery can last over 150 hours on a single charge.
Can I customize the RGB lighting?
Yes — our companion software gives you full control over colors, effects, and profiles. You can save presets, sync lighting across devices, and even set lighting to respond to game audio.
What is the warranty and support like?
Dopple includes a 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects and hardware failures. If you need help, our support team provides live chat and email support 24/7.
What accessories are included?
Each kit includes a braided USB-C cable, 4 sets of replacement PTFE glide feet, and a padded carrying pouch. Extra weights and grips are available as optional add-ons.
